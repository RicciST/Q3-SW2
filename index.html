5 of My Favorite Comfort Movies



1. Juno
Director: Jason Reitman
Date Released (Philippines): February 13, 2008
Synopsis: High school student Juno Macgruff gets pregnant with her schoolmate Paulie Bleeker's child. Through the newspaper, Juno chooses a family to adopt the child after she gives birth, but major personal complications come along the way.


2. Herb
Director: In-mu Heo
Date Released (South Korea): January 11, 2007
Synopsis: A young woman with a developmental disability discovers and navigates romantic love as well as a loss in her family.


3. The Hunger Games
Directors: Francis Lawrence & Gary Ross
Date Released (Philippines): March 22, 2012
Synopsis: In a dystopian world, Katniss Everdeen volunteers to participate in the Hunger Games as a tribute. During the game, she learns to rely on her survival and combat skills.


4. To All the Boys: P.S. I Still Love You
Directors: Michael Fimognari
Date Released: February 12, 2020
Synopsis: Lara Jean and her boyfriend Peter are doing very well in their relationship until John Ambrose, a former crush of Lara Jean, reconnects with her. This new friendship threatens the stability of Lara Jean and Peter's relationship.


5. Violet Evergarden: Eternity and the Auto Memory Doll
Director: Haruka Fujita
Date Released (Japan): September 6, 2019
Synopsis: Violet Evergarden tutors Amy Bartlett, a student studying at a private women's academy. Amy feels detached at this academy until Violet turns up.
